PART A - IDENTIFY STUDY ARMS (HMD - Columns):
Find the comparison groups reported in this study with HIERARCHICAL structure:
- Each study arm is a PARENT with optional CHILDREN sub-columns
- PARENT: Group name eng sample size (e.g., "Low-risk pT1 (N=70)")
- CHILDREN: Sub-columns for that group (if data is broken down further OR has specific descriptors)

  * Subgroup classifications
  * Inclusion Criteria (e.g., "pT1 without risk factors")
  * Time points
  * Or empty [] if no sub-columns needed
- Statistical columns (P-Value, OR, HR) typically have NO children
- **IMPORTANT**: If a group has descriptive text lines in the header, make each line a CHILD column.
